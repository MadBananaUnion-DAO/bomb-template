BananaBombBattles-inc: Discord Clicking Game

Overview:

BananaBombBattles-inc presents an exciting clicking game set in the vibrant world of Discord. Join the battle as a "Bomber" or a "Blocker" and compete against other players to accumulate points and emerge victorious! Strategic decisions, teamwork, and resource management are key to success in this fast-paced game.

Game Mechanics:

  • Players choose roles: "Bomber" or "Blocker".
  • 180-second battle duration.
  • Perform actions using $BOMB currency.
  • Bombers bomb targets; Blockers defend against attacks.
  • Earn $BOMB for successful actions.
  • Limited actions per player.

How to Play:

  1. Join the BananaBombBattles-inc Discord server.
  2. Choose your role: Bomber or Blocker.
  3. Collect $BOMB currency every 6 hours.
  4. Participate in battles and earn points for your team.
  5. Strategize, click, and dominate to emerge victorious!
